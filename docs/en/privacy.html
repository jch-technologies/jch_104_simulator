The operator of this app takes the protection of your personal data very seriously. We treat your personal data confidentially and in accordance with statutory data protection regulations and this privacy policy.

Note on the Responsible Party

The responsible party for data processing is indicated in the legal notice.

3. Data Collection in the App

What Data is Collected?

This app stores the following data exclusively locally on your device:

  • Connection settings (IP addresses, ports)
  • Saved connection profiles
  • App settings (language, accent color)
  • Data points in server mode
  • Protocol logs during use

Is Data Transferred to Third Parties?

No. This app does not transfer any data to us or third parties. The only network communication occurs directly between your device and the remote station (PLC, RTU, SCADA system) you configure using the IEC 60870-5-104 protocol.

IEC 60870-5-104 Communication

This app implements the IEC 60870-5-104 protocol for communication with industrial control systems. Communication occurs:

  • Via TCP/IP on the configured port (default: 2404)
  • Directly between your device and the remote station
  • Without intermediate storage on external servers
  • Without encryption (as specified in the IEC 104 standard)
